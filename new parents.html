OVERVIEW

This project explores the design of a digital health record system that helps first-time mothers track both their own health and their baby’s well-being. The goal is to create a seamless connection between mother and child’s health data, allowing users to identify patterns, receive insights, and stay informed about their postpartum and newborn care.

This case study presents a low-fidelity prototype of both a mobile application and a web-based interface, designed to provide a user-friendly and intuitive experience for new parents navigating early childcare.
